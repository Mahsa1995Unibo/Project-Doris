Our Conclusion

The best approach is Chain-of-Thought + RAG — combining structured KG triples with step-by-step LLM reasoning. This produces the most accurate, deep, and trustworthy analysis. The weakest approach is LLM-only with no triples: it sounds convincing but describes general historical knowledge rather than our specific corpus.

Most important finding
When given triples from Nazi-era German newspapers, both GPT-4o and Claude add modern moral framing not present in the original data. This is LLM bias injection — the models cannot help but contextualise antisemitic framing through a contemporary ethical lens. This is useful to document: it shows the gap between what the KG says and what the LLM interprets.
